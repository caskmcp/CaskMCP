# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to making participation in CaskMCP a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity/expression, level of experience, nationality, race, religion, or sexual identity/orientation.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, direct, and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but prioritize clear technical reasoning.
- Give actionable feedback focused on code, docs, and behavior.
- Accept and act on maintainers' moderation decisions.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, intimidation, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or imagery.
- Doxxing, stalking, or unwanted contact.
- Deliberate disruption, trolling, or bad-faith participation.
- Publishing private information (credentials, tokens, personal data) without explicit permission.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, reviews, and any other CaskMCP-managed communication channels.

## Reporting and enforcement

- Report incidents to maintainers using a private-first channel.
- If you do not have a private channel, follow the process in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md): request a private follow-up and keep sensitive details out of public threads.
- Maintainers may remove comments, lock threads, reject contributions, or temporarily/permanently ban participants for violations.
